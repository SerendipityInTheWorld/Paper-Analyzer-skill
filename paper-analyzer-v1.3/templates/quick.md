# Quick Scan Template
Use when `--mode=quick`. Each dimension gets exactly 1-2 lines.

- **Metadata**: {title} | {authors} | {venue/ year}
- **Motivation**: {one-sentence problem background}
- **Problem**: {the specific gap addressed}
- **Contributions**: {list, comma-separated}
- **Method Innovation**: {one-line essence}
- **Theory**: {key theoretical basis}
- **Experiments**: {N experiments covering: core, ablation, qualitative, robustness}
- **Key Results**: {most important finding with number}
- **Limitations**: {top 2}
- **Future**: {suggested direction}
- **Assessment**: {strong / moderate / weak — one word on evidence quality}
